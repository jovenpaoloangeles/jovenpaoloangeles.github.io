About

My doctoral thesis develops a Bayesian optimization framework for accelerated nanoparticle synthesis — fewer experiments, lower cost, and results other labs can reproduce. The same instinct drives my engineering work: at DOST-ASTI I lead JuanaKNOW, a multi-agent platform for IP licensing, startup funding, and technology transfer, built on Agno, Ollama, and Postgres.

Earlier I built the data side: an ELT pipeline for public-sector documents with LLM-assisted parsing and deduplication, hybrid retrieval over pgvector, and a unified databank exposed through WSO2.

Nights and weekends belong to a home lab that trains trading models on a schedule, runs federated-learning experiments, and quietly replaces a stack of commercial subscriptions — plus PRINT3D.MNL, a 3D printing studio for computational design.
